The following resources provide free tiers, developer-friendly APIs, or standardized RSS feeds as of 2026.

1. Core News & Geopolitics (Aggregators)
These APIs act as the "engine" of your app, allowing you to filter millions of articles by specific keywords like "NATO," "Semiconductor Supply Chain," or "South China Sea."

Provider	Free Tier Details	Best For
NewsData.io	200 credits/day	High-reliability global news. Supports 80+ languages and granular country filtering.
GNews API	100 requests/day	Simple REST API for searching current and historical news from 60,000+ sources.
NewsAPI.org	1,000 requests/day	The industry standard for developers; best for quickly fetching headlines from major outlets.
Mediastack	100 requests/month	Lightweight JSON API for live news. Good for "breaking news" tickers.

3. Financial & Economic Data (API)
For your app to be "expert grade," you should include hard economic indicators (inflation, interest rates, currency volatility).

Alpha Vantage: A very generous free tier for daily stock and forex data, plus economic indicators (GDP, CPI).

FRED API: Run by the St. Louis Fed. It is completely free and provides 800,000+ economic data series (e.g., debt-to-GDP ratios, unemployment).

Twelve Data: Good for real-time market data with a credit-based free tier.
